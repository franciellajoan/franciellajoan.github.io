Equilateral Triangle

A triangle in which all three sides are of equal length. In such a triangle, the angles are all equal as well.


Isosceles Triangle

A triangle with (at least) two sides of equal length, and therefore also with (at least) two equal angles.


Parallelogram

A quadrilateral with opposite sides parallel and therefore opposite angles equal.


Polygon

A two-dimensional figure that consists of a collection of line segments, joined at their ends.


Quadrilateral

A four-sided polygon.


Rectangle

A quadrilateral with opposite sides of equal lengths, and with four right angles.


Regular Polygon

A polygon in which the sides are all the same length and the angles all have the same measure.


Right Triangle

A triangle that has a right angle. The Pythagorean Theorem is a relationship among the sides of a right triangle.


Square

A polygon with four sides of equal length and at right angles to each other.


Trapezoid

A quadrilateral with two sides parallel.


Triangle

A three-sided (and three-angled) polygon.
