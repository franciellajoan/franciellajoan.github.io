Why was this website created?

The purpose of this website is for students to learn more about mathematics, not only those who excel in the said subject, but also those who are struggling with it. As a Pisay scholar, I know the head-throbbing difficulty of Mathematics, especially as it progress throughout the year.

What was my inspiration to make this website?

Well, since Jonyle wanted a "mathematical-themed" website, here I am, creating it. Although I'm not that fond with the given theme, I did my best in making this a very user-friendly and visually appealing website for a perfect grade in this project, or a 1 in Computer Science 3.

How was this website created?

Well, coders usually run on coffee, and I'm not an exception to that. This website was made with a lot of coffee, procrastination, cramming, research, cheat sheets, brute force, help from the Comp Sci Lords, Stack Overflow, food, background music, motivation, will, and inspiration.

Will I be creating more websites like this?

Seeing how hard HTML and CSS is, it makes me think twice. It depends if it's needed for my grades or no. Just kidding. Of course! I would very much like to continue making websites. Who knows, maybe I'll become a website developer in the future!
